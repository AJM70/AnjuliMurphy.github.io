About Me

I am an experienced data analyst who is passionate about enabling data-driven decision making through actionable insights. My motivation is turning data into something meaningful that has real impact. I design and construct data solutions to solve business intelligence challenges, including data visualisation, dashboards, reports and MI.

Highlights:

  • I have enjoyed successes over 14 years in data roles, including working directly with top UK banks and insurers whilst supporting SaaS (Software as a Service) in fraud prevention.
  • I have extensive experience in financial services and insurance. I understand the different types of products, data, and processes involved. As Data Insight Team Leader I was responsible for client customer backbook screening for regulatory and compliance purposes, including data from third party providers.
  • I am proactive and take the initiative. Although I've moved away from managing people to focus on BI challenges, I see myself as retaining leader qualities in how I conduct my work. I am self-motivated and work well alone, but also appreciate fundamentally how working collaboratively and closely with others brings the best results in BI.
